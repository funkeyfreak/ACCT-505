Flemming Company


The following expenditures relating to plant assets were made by Flemming Company during the first 2 month of 20XX.

  1. Paid $5600 for installation of new factory equipment.
  2. Paid $950 sales tax on a new delivery van.
  3. Paid $8,200 of accrued taxes at the same time the plant site was acquired.
  4. Paid $500 insurance to cover a possible accident loss on new factory equipment while the machinery was in transit.
  5. Paid $800 to have the company name and slogan painted.
  6. Paid $21,000 for parking lots and driveways on the new plant site.
  7. Paid $940 for a 1-year accident insurance policy on the new delivery van.
  8. Paid $110 Motor vehicle license fee on the new van.

Instructions:
  • For each transaction:
    1. Indicate whether it is a capital expenditure or an operating expense.
    2. Indicate the account title to which each expenditure should be debited.
