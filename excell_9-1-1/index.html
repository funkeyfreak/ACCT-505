Supplemental Exercise 9.1.1

Willow Corporation


The following expenditures relating to plant assets were made by Willow Corporation during the first month of the current year.

  1. Paid $ 6,000 of accrued taxes at the time the plant site was acquired.
  2. Paid $ 100 insurance to cover a possible accident loss on new factory machinery while the machinery was in transit.
  3. Paid $ 1,050 sales taxes on a new delivery truck.
  4. Paid $ 2,000 for parking lots and driveways on the new plant site.
  5. Paid $ 280 to have the company name and slogan painted on the new delivery truck.
  6. Paid $ 5,000 for installation of new factory machinery.
  7. Paid $ 700 for a 1- year accident insurance policy on the new delivery truck.
  8. Paid $ 95 motor vehicle license fee on the new truck.

Instructions:
  • Record these transactions in the general journal of Willow Corporation.
